# SEO Optimizer Skill for RennAsks

This skill guides the SEOAgent in writing search-optimized, high-performance video metadata, tags, and design briefs for YouTube that strictly respect the `RennAsks` tone.

## Keyword Extraction Instructions
1. **Primary Keyword Identifier:** Extract the core concept from the video script (e.g., "quantum computing" or "student debt"). This must appear in the description.
2. **Secondary Keyword Extraction:** Identify 3-5 helper search terms or related topics from the script to naturally distribute throughout the description and tags.

## Title Formula Guidelines & Examples
Titles must align with the `RennAsks` channel tone: strict lowercase preference, direct, zero hype, under 60 characters.

### RennAsks Title Formulas
*   **The Quantitative Metric:** Include concrete numbers.
    *   *why 90% of college degrees are useless now*
    *   *5 facts about the quantum computing threat*
*   **The Direct Question:** Formulate a blunt question.
    *   *are they lying to you about quantum encryption?*
    *   *why is your landlord getting richer off your rent?*
*   **The Blunt Reality:** A statement of fact.
    *   *the actual cost of renting your life in 2026*
    *   *how qubits break your security systems*

### Banned Title Types
- **Hype/Caps Clickbait:** *YOU WON'T BELIEVE WHAT HAPPENS NEXT!!! 😱*
- **Generic corporate title:** *An Introduction to Advanced Quantum Computing Paradigms*

## Tag Strategy
Build the tags list utilizing three levels:
1. **Broad (2-3 tags):** Main parent niche (e.g., `technology`, `finance`).
2. **Specific (4-6 tags):** Target subject area (e.g., `quantum computing`, `student loans`).
3. **Long-tail (4-6 tags):** Search queries/phrases (e.g., `is quantum computing real`, `why college is a scam`).
